The Daily Love was created with the aim of being a “place to love and be loved.”

Sounds simple, right? But for Mastin Kipp, creator of Daily Love, this simple concept of

having something you could relate and turn to was what kept him going when he lost

doing a damn good job achieving its goal.

burning those calories. We love it. So definitely don't miss Blogilates.com!
